Trossachs Ski School

Develop your skiing in style

Situated in the beautiful Scottish Trossachs, we are a ski school dedicated to empowering and developing ski enthusiasts in Scotland. Learning with us, you can benefit from industry-leading instruction and lesson plans which are tailored to your requirements. We provide tuition to skiers of all levels, abilities and ages at competitive prices.

Reasons to ski with us

Expert Instructors

All of our instructors are qualified at BASI Level 2 or equivalent and have several years of teaching experience.

Comprehensive Tuition

Our lesson plans are recognsied and accredited by the International Ski Instructor Association.

Competitive Pricing

We offer a wide range of fairly priced options for all levels and learning preferences.

Improve Your Fitness

Skiing is a high performance sport and a great way for you to develop and maintain your fitness.

Lessons

Ski School

Dedicated day-long instruction for kids and young adults. Bring your kids to collection point at 9.45am, return by 3pm. Perfect for couples with kids looking for some time to ski themselves!

  • Group size: up to 12
  • Age: 5-16
  • Level: Beginner - Advanced

£70 per day, £300 for 5-day week

Individual private

One-to-one private lessons with one of our instructors. You can choose what you want to learn if you already have something in mind. We typically recommend booking a half-day lesson to get the most out of it.

  • Group size: 1
  • Age: 16+
  • Level: Any

£25 per hour, £100 per full day

Group private

You can also book private lessons for groups of up to 4 people. We recommend participants being relatively close in ability so that all can benefit.

  • Group size: 2-4
  • Age: 16+
  • Level: Any

£50 per group per hour, £200 per full day

Private ski tour

Keen to explore the backcountry routes? Look no further! One of our experienced guides will be able to show you around some of the lesser known routes in the Trossachs area - and you can even visit our hut, if you’re up for a demanding trek!

  • Group size: 2-6
  • Age: 16+
  • Level: Intermediate and up

£300 for group day tour

Clinics

Carving Clinic

Learn how to get low… This clinic is all about performance skiing and carving. Your trainer will take you on a 5-day intensive course which is totally dedicated to improving your carving technique, increasing your stability at speed, and dialling up your edge angle.

  • Level: Advanced
  • Next date: 6-10 January 2025

£500 per person, full week

Freestyle Clinic

Let’s face it, jumps and tricks are pretty cool… but rather scary! This clinic offers a great way for you to take the plunge into 5 days of freestyle training with some of our steeziest trainers. Flips, 180/360s, boxes, rails, and more - you’ll cover them all here.

  • Level: Expert
  • Next date: 20-24 January 2025

£400 per person, full week

Moguls Clinic

Bumps - everyone hates them until they don't. Learn how to manage, absorb, and cut through mogul-fields in this physically intensive clinic. You’ll start with some varied terrain and progress onto zipper-lines and true moguls. This is a great way to improve your stability, balance and body position in other areas of skiing.

  • Level: Expert
  • Next date: 3-7 February 2025

£450 per person, full week

Offpiste Clinic

Few things are better than being able to cut new lines in fresh snow. With this clinic, you’ll learn how to tackle offpiste terrain and deeper, more varied snowpacks in style. You’ll also become an expert on avalanche awareness and safety, utilising cutting-edge equipment to ensure your future safety.

  • Level: Advanced
  • Next date: 17-21 February 2025

£500 per person, full week
