Trossachs Ski School

Develop your skiing in style!

Situated in the beautiful Scottish Trossachs, we are a newly formed ski school dedicated to empowering and developing ski enthusiasts in Scotland. Learning with us, you can benefit from industry-leading instruction and lesson plans which are tailored to your requirements. We provide tuition to skiers of all levels, abilities and ages at competitive prices.

Reasons to ski with us

  • Expert Instructors: All of our instructors are qualified at BASI Level 2 or equivalent and have several years of teaching experience.

  • Comprehensive Tuition: Our lesson plans are recognsied and accredited by the International Ski Instructor Association.

  • Competitive Pricing: We offer a wide range of fairly priced options for all levels and learning preferences.

Lessons

Ski School

Dedicated day-long instruction for kids and young adults. Bring your kids to collection point at 9.45am, return by 3pm. Perfect for couples with kids looking for some time to ski themselves!

  • Group size: up to 12
  • Age: 5-16
  • Level: Beginner to Intermediate

£70 per day, £300 for 5-day week

Individual private

One-to-one private lessons with one of our instructors. You can choose what you want to learn if you already have something in mind. We typically recommend booking a half-day lesson to get the most out of it.

  • Group size: 1
  • Age: 16+
  • Level: Any

£25 per hour, with discounts for half- or full-day bookings

Group private

You can also book private lessons for groups of up to 4 people. We recommend participants being relatively close in ability so that all can benefit.

  • Group size: 2-4
  • Age: 16+
  • Level: Any

£50 per group hour, with discounts for half- or full-day bookings

Private ski tour

Keen to explore the backcountry routes? Look no further! One of our experienced guides will be able to show you around some of the lesser known routes in the Trossachs area - and you can even visit our hut, if you’re up for a demanding trek!

  • Group size: 2-6
  • Age: 16+
  • Level: Intermediate or above

£300 for group day tour
